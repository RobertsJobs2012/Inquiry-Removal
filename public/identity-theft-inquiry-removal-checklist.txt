Identity Theft Inquiry Removal Checklist

Use this checklist before disputing hard inquiries that appeared because of identity theft.

1. Pull all three credit reports: Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion.
2. List every hard inquiry you do not recognize.
3. File a free FTC identity theft report at IdentityTheft.gov.
4. Save the FTC Identity Theft Report PDF.
5. Consider filing a local police report for additional documentation.
6. Place a credit freeze with Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion.
7. Place a fraud alert, or an extended fraud alert if you have an FTC identity theft report.
8. Review your reports for fraudulent accounts in addition to fraudulent inquiries.
9. Dispute each fraudulent inquiry using your FTC report as supporting documentation.

This checklist is informational only and is not legal advice.
